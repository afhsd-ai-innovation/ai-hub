Example Use Cases

Need some inspiration on how to get started? Explore these common ways staff are using AI Assistants to streamline their work and improve outcomes.

Email and Message Drafting

Keep your tone, structure, and phrasing consistent across follow-ups, updates, or client communication. Perfect for maintaining a professional voice without the effort.

Document Summaries

Apply the same to reports, meeting notes, or research papers for uniform outputs. Quickly distill long documents into actionable bullet points.

Content Planning

Turn an editorial calendar or idea sheet into structured outlines, suggested headlines, or prioritized drafts. Great for social media or newsletter planning.

Knowledge Base Q&A

Connect to product docs, FAQs, or training manuals so answers stay accurate and repeatable. Turn your static PDFs into interactive experts.

Research Assistant

Extract key themes from uploaded articles or papers without having to rewrite prompts for every new source. Ideal for academic or administrative research.

Meeting Prep and Debriefs

Use consistent formats for agendas, action items, and summaries across different teams or clients. Ensure nothing gets lost in the shuffle.

Data Interpretation

Upload spreadsheets or survey results and always get structured insights, highlights, and recommendations. Make sense of data in seconds.

Onboarding Helpers

Create an assistant that introduces new employees to company tools, values, and workflows in a repeatable way. A 24/7 mentor for new staff.

Project Management Support

Generate task lists, status updates, and risk summaries in the same structure each time. Keep projects moving forward with consistent reporting.

Creative Coaching

Save a “voice coach” or “style guide” to refine writing, design drafts, or brainstorming sessions with consistency. Your personal editor and creative partner.
